Meeting Details
QBR Agenda & Working Notes

Recommended total: 60 minutes. Click each section to expand and add notes.

Discussion Points
  • ›Introduce attendees; confirm agenda & time
  • ›Re-align on partner's top business priorities for H2
  • ›Review mutual success criteria & partnership goals
  • ›GoCanvas APAC updates: new features, roadmap highlights
Review Checklist
  • ›Revenue & pipeline vs target — walk through scorecard
  • ›New logos won; deals lost — root cause analysis
  • ›Customer health & retention rate review
  • ›Certification & training status
  • ›Deal registration compliance
  • ›Celebrate wins & acknowledge effort
Explore
  • ›Competitive pressures: what are you hearing in the field?
  • ›Technical or integration blockers
  • ›Pricing & positioning friction in market
  • ›Support or escalation issues
  • ›Resourcing constraints on partner side
What to cover
  • ›Share a customer success story: productivity/efficiency gain
  • ›Reference GoCanvas stat: avg $52K annual time savings
  • ›Highlight 34% avg productivity increase at mutual customers
  • ›Align on 1–2 shared customer case studies for co-marketing
Plan & Commit
  • ›Set Q3 pipeline & revenue targets (SMART)
  • ›Agree on co-sell motions & joint campaigns
  • ›Identify top 5 target accounts for focused pursuit
  • ›Enablement needs: training, collateral, demos
  • ›GoCanvas product roadmap preview (if NDA'd)
Before Closing
  • ›Read back all action items with owner & due date
  • ›Confirm next QBR / check-in date
  • ›Ask: "What's one thing GoCanvas can do better?"
  • ›Send recap email within 24 hours
